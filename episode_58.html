Episode 58: Hannah Morgan

Episode Notes

In this episode, we chat with Hannah Morgan, a working mom who has managed to blend career, family, and entrepreneurship in a way that’s both inspiring and practical. Hannah is not only a project manager in the fintech space but also the founder of Heron House Management, a virtual house management service designed to help busy parents lighten their mental load. With three young children—ages 4, 3, and 6 months—Hannah is no stranger to the juggling act that comes with balancing work, family, and personal goals.
She shares her passion for helping families reclaim their time and energy so they can fully embrace life’s beautiful, chaotic moments without feeling overwhelmed by the constant barrage of tasks. Hannah also talks about how she manages to balance her career, her growing business, and her role as a mother while still maintaining her personal identity and goals.
To learn more about Hannah's business, Heron House Management check out her website! You can find her timesaving tools at her Etsy shop!
