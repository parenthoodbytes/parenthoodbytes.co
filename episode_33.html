Episode 33: Vera Mehta

Episode Notes

In this episode, we chat with Vera Mehta, the Senior Director of Software Engineering at ClearScore in the UK. Vera, who is a dedicated mother to a 14-month-old son, opens up about her journey navigating the professional landscape while pregnant. She candidly discusses the ongoing struggles she faces, including the delicate task of disclosing her pregnancy to prospective employers and the importance of feeling valued in the workplace.
Vera sheds light on the nuances of parental leave policies in the UK, offering valuable perspectives on how companies can better support their employees during this crucial time. She also shares the personal challenges of re-entering the workforce after parental leave and the perpetual juggle of adapting to accommodate a growing child.
Through Vera's honest reflections, listeners gain valuable insights into the intricacies of balancing a thriving career with the joys and responsibilities of motherhood. Tune in for a thoughtful exploration of how Vera successfully manages the demands of both worlds.
