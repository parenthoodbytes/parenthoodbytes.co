Episode 1: Jon

Jon is a Senior Software Engineer, currently working as a Consultant. He has two kids ages 8 and 10. In this episode, he talks about the differences of his first parental leave at his company as well as the things his wife and he do to get things done. His tip for new parents is to be cautious of and do what you can to avoid burnout.

Episode 2: Abdul

Abdul is an IT manager in Ireland, who juggles a demanding career at a large tech company while parenting two little ones, aged 4 and 2. Abdul shares invaluable insights for new parents and fellow parents in tech, urging them not to shy away from discussing parental leave - communication is key. Abdul advocates being open with your manager and team, while also encouraging parents to ask as many questions as they need to feel supported and empowered. He also talks about the importance of taking personal time off - not just for the family but for yourself as well. He highlights the need to avoid the dreaded burnout that so often lurks in the background.

Episode 3: Julie Hollek

In this episode of Parenthood Bytes, we meet Julie Hollek, an inspiring mother and Director of Data Science at Mozilla, currently based in Texas. Julie shares her incredible journey as the primary breadwinner while raising two children, aged 11 and 17 months old, and how she successfully manages the responsibilities in collaboration with her partner.

Julie's story unfolds as she navigates the intricate balance of parenting, professional growth, and her pursuit of knowledge, while raising a toddler and working in the dynamic tech industry. She provides valuable insights and advice for parents, emphasizing the importance of not letting fear hold you back. Julie encourages parents to understand that while there is never a "perfect time" to have kids, with resilience and determination, you can overcome any challenge that comes your way.

Bobby provides a candid look into the challenges and rewards of parenthood, offering his experience and wisdom.
