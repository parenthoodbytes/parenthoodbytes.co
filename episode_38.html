Episode 38: Chris Guillory

Episode Notes

In this episode, we sit down with Chris Guillory, a devoted father and Project Manager at Modo Health. He talks about his journey and insights on navigating career shifts and parenting as a single dad.
Chris opens up about his transition from the financial tech sector to medical tech, offering valuable perspectives on adapting to new industries within the tech realm. As a single parent to two boys, aged 18 and 13, he candidly shares his strategies for balancing the demands of work and family life.
One of the key themes Chris discusses is the importance of being present both at work and at home. He emphasizes that while professional roles may be replaceable, the moments and connections with loved ones are irreplaceable.
