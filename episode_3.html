Episode 3: Julie Hollek

Episode Notes

In this episode of Parenthood Bytes, we meet Julie Hollek, an inspiring mother and Director of Data Science at Mozilla, currently based in Texas. Julie shares her incredible journey as the primary breadwinner while raising two children, aged 11 and 17 months old, and how she successfully manages the responsibilities in collaboration with her partner.
Julie's story unfolds as she navigates the intricate balance of parenting, professional growth, and her pursuit of knowledge, while raising a toddler and working in the dynamic tech industry. She provides valuable insights and advice for parents, emphasizing the importance of not letting fear hold you back. Julie encourages parents to understand that while there is never a "perfect time" to have kids, with resilience and determination, you can overcome any challenge that comes your way.
