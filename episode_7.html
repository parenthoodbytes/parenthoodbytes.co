Episode 7: Will Ramirez

Episode Notes

In this episode, we engage in a conversation with Will Ramirez, a Senior Software Engineer at Mongo DB, currently residing in Florida. Will generously shares his experiences of raising a 6-year-old and a 17-year-old, offering valuable insights for parents navigating the challenges of family life.
Will's advice serves as a universal truth for all parents: no matter how meticulously you plan your life, having kids introduces an element of unpredictability. Drawing a parallel to planning in the workplace, Will highlights that just as one plans for the future in their professional life, unexpected events and changes are inevitable. Parenting, like work planning, requires adaptability and flexibility as circumstances evolve.
Tune in to gain perspective from Will's journey, discovering how adaptability and a resilient mindset play crucial roles in successfully navigating the dynamic landscape of parenthood.
