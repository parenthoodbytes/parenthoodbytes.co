Episode 39: Monique van den Berg

Episode Notes

In this episode, we speak with Monique van den Berg, Head of Online Community & Community Management at Atlassian. Monique shares her unique journey into tech, highlighting her intentional approach to weekly planning and the importance of taking vacations even if you aren't planning to physically go anywhere. As a mother to a 13-year-old daughter, she emphasizes the value of validating teen emotions while encouraging them with her belief in their potential. Tune in for insights on career transitions, work-life balance, and parenting.
