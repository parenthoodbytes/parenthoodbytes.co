Parenthood Bytes

A Podcast about Parents in Tech

Join us on the "Parenthood Bytes" podcast, where we explore the dynamic journey of parenthood within the fast-paced world of technology. In each episode, we sit down with a different parent who's navigating the intersection of family and a tech career. We dive deep into their personal path to tech, sharing their challenges, triumphs, and invaluable insights.
Discover the stories of these remarkable parents as they discuss their unique journeys, from the early days of breaking into the tech industry to the daily balancing act of work and family life. We explore how they maintain their sanity while juggling the demands of coding, meetings, and naptime.
Work/life balance is a constant challenge, and we uncover the strategies and secrets these parents use to achieve it. From setting boundaries to time management tips, they offer practical advice that can benefit all tech-loving moms and dads.
But it's not all smooth sailing, and we don't shy away from discussing the hurdles these parents have overcome. From sleepless nights to deadlines, we dig into the struggles and how they managed to triumph over them.
Are you a parent in the tech industry, or aspiring to be? "Parenthood Bytes" provides a wealth of knowledge and a supportive community. Join us for engaging conversations, inspiring stories, and actionable tips to help you thrive both in your career and as a parent. Tune in to "Parenthood Bytes" and become part of a network that understands your unique journey."
