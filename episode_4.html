Episode 4: Bobby

Episode Notes

In this episode, we sit down with Bobby, a Senior Project Manager at Amazon, currently based in Texas. Bobby shares the highs, lows, and the sheer joy of raising his two kids, aged 12 and 10.
Bobby provides a candid look into the challenges and rewards of parenthood, offering his experience and wisdom. His practical tip for fellow parents is: anticipate and savor the significant changes that parenthood brings. While vacationing with kids is entirely possible, Bobby emphasizes the importance of taking vacation time with just your partner.
Furthermore, he advocates for a balanced perspective on work, suggesting that finding a job that prioritizes family can make all the difference. As he aptly puts it, "don't take work so seriously."
