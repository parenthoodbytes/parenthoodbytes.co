Episode 23: Karthik Sethuramen

Episode Notes

In this episode, we chat with Karthik Sethuramen, a seasoned Staff Software Engineer based in the Bay Area. Despite the demands of his career, Karthik generously shares his experiences and strategies for maintaining a balance between work and family life, particularly with a lively 3.5-year-old son.
Karthik provides a candid glimpse into the dynamic of being a dual-career household with a young child. He discusses the challenges he and his wife face as they navigate their busy schedules while ensuring their son receives the attention and engagement he deserves. From managing hectic workdays to orchestrating family activities, Karthik sheds light on modern parenthood.
A standout takeaway from Karthik's insights is the importance of community support. He emphasizes the invaluable role that a community plays in their parenting journey.
