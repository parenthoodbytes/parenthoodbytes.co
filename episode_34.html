Episode 34: David Spark

Episode Notes

In this episode, we sit down with David Spark, co-host, producer, and managing editor of the CISO Series, a media network for cybersecurity professionals. David, who lives in Southern California, has two children, ages 10 and 13, shares his insights on managing his own business while raising a family.
David discusses the importance of setting boundaries between work and personal life and how he prioritizes family time with intentionality. He offers his top tip for parents: take a daily video of your children. This practice creates cherished memories and provides a meaningful gift for them as they grow older.
For more on the CISO Series, visit cisoseries.com. To learn about VidCon, happening on June 26-29, 2024, in Anaheim, CA, visit vidcon.com.
