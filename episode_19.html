Episode 19: Andrew Tio

Episode Notes

In this episode, we sit down with Andrew Tio, a Staff Frontend Engineer at Proper and a devoted father of two young girls, aged 3 and a newborn (as of the date this episode is published). His second daughter was born on January 9!
Andrew shares insights into his experience managing the delicate balance between work and family life. Despite the challenges, Andrew emphasizes the importance of being intentional about dedicating quality time to kids outside of work commitments. He also stresses the significance of carving out moments for "me" time when possible, while not taking time away from work or kids. Andrew advocates for remote work as a valuable option for parents, highlighting its potential to facilitate a healthier work-life dynamic. He candidly acknowledges the difficulties of parenting, underscoring the necessity of recognizing and verbalizing the challenges in raising children. Ultimately, Andrew emphasizes the importance of adaptability and spontaneity in navigating the complexities of parenthood, as much as one may strive to plan ahead.
