Episode 80: Lisa Schultz - Journey into Tech and Raising Young Kids Through Career Transitions

Episode Notes

In this episode, we chat with Lisa Shultz, who is a seasoned Marketing Operations professional with more than 15 years of experience in the fast-paced world of high-growth startups. A mother of two (ages 6 and 11), Lisa currently leads a home organizing and consulting agency, drawing upon her extensive marketing operations background. In this conversation, she recounts her journey into tech, her evolution into marketing, and the story behind her entrepreneurial venture. Lisa also openly shares her experience taking a deliberate step away from tech and her current desire to re-enter the field.
