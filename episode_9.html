Episode 9: Pedro Rico

Episode Notes

In this episode, we sit down with Pedro Rico, a seasoned Staff Software Engineer in the music streaming industry with 16-17 years of experience. Alongside his tech career, Pedro is also a dedicated parent to a 6-year-old son.
Pedro candidly shares his most significant parental challengeâ€”navigating the delicate balance between ambitious career goals and family life. Pedro talks about how he manages to juggle both aspects of his life while striving to maintain a healthy work-life balance.
Pedro offers a practical tip for those contemplating parenthood. He suggests that if you have a penchant for travel or enjoy adult-oriented experiences such as museum visits, it's advisable to indulge in these before starting a family. However, for parents who embark on adventures with young kids, Pedro provides a checklist of essential items for a smooth journey, including snacks, extra diapers, a car seat, items to help children adjust their ears, and of course extra clothing for you and your children!
Tune in to hear more about Pedro's experiences and struggles managing life as a parent and as a parent in tech.
