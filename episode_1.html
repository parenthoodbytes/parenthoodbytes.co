Episode 1: Jon

Episode Notes

Jon is a Staff Software Engineer, currently working as a Consultant. He has two kids ages 8 and 10. In this episode, he talks about the differences of his first parental leave at his company as well as the things his wife and he do to get things done. His tip for new parents is to be cautious of and do what you can to avoid burnout.
