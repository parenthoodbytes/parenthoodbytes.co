Episode 35: Ebenezer Anjorin

Episode Notes

In this episode, we sit down with Ebenezer Anjorin, a Product Manager at Google, who shares his experiences balancing a demanding career with family life. With a son turning three and another baby on the way, he discusses the boundaries he had to establish upon becoming a parent, his strategies for maintaining these boundaries, and how he manages the daily juggle of work and family responsibilities.
Ebenezer candidly explains that while balancing these aspects of life isn't easy, you eventually figure out a rhythm. He emphasizes that though it's a lot of hard work, the rewards are immense, providing a deep sense of purpose and fulfillment.
Tune in to hear his story!
