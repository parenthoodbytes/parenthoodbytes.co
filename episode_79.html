Episode 79: Nam - Balancing a Career & Raising Young Kids

Episode Notes

In this episode, we're delighted to continue our conversation with Nam, a Director of Product at Oracle and a dedicated father to two young children, ages one and four. For those who tuned in to episode 78, you'll recall Nam's inspiring journey into the tech industry and his thoughtful approach to career transitions while embarking on the adventure of starting a family. Now, Nam returns to offer invaluable insights into his approach to work/life balance, sharing his personal strategies for effectively managing his demanding time. He also opens up about his perspective on his own ongoing career development as he navigates the rewarding yet challenging years of raising young toddlers.
