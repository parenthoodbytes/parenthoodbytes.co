Episode 16: Omar McNeil

Episode Notes

In this podcast episode, we interview Omar McNeil, a Staff L&D Program Manager at Github based in North Carolina, who also runs a YouTube channel, @omarvelouslyblerdy12. Omar, a father of two (ages 10 and 7), shares valuable insights about balancing work and family life, particularly emphasizing the importance of thoroughly assessing job benefits.
Omar highlights the significance of considering the entire job package, especially benefits, when evaluating a new position. He stresses the need for planning when going on parental leave, ensuring tasks are delegated, and that leadership has the necessary resources in place during one's absence.
Time management emerges as a key theme in Omar's discussion, advocating for time-blocking schedules and effective delegation to navigate the demands of both work and parenting. He acknowledges the occasional necessity of saying no and the importance of maintaining a joint family calendar to organize activities and tasks.
For new parents, Omar offers practical advice, urging them to be patient and embrace the learning curve. Supporting each other and finding joy in the parenting journey are also crucial aspects, as parenthood is a wild and crazy ride that should be savored despite its challenges. Omar's parting advice is to enjoy the moments, as time flies by quickly.
You can find Omar's channel at https://www.youtube.com/@omarvelouslyblerdy12.
