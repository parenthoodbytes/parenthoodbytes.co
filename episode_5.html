Episode 5: Song

Episode Notes

This episode features an insightful conversation with Song, the VP of Technical Services at Nium, residing in the Bay Area. With almost 25 years of experience in the tech industry and a 17-year-old son, Song shares valuable perspectives on successful parenting in the tech world. The key lies in effective communication with both your company and your partner.
Song highlights the significance of achieving a balance with your partner when it comes to roles and responsibilities in parenting. He underscores the importance of open and frequent communication, emphasizing that there is no one-size-fits-all blueprint for success. What works for one family may not work for another, and vice versa. The crucial aspect is to identify and implement what is best for your own family dynamics.
