Episode 21: Anna Sulkina

Episode Notes

In this episode, Anna Sulkina, Director of Engineering at Airbnb and mother of 15-year-old twins, shares her insights on parenting and balancing a demanding career. With over 25 years of tech experience, Anna offers valuable advice for parents raising twins. She emphasizes the importance of syncing their schedules, suggesting that when one wakes up to feed at night, it's best to feed both to establish consistency.
Anna also highlights the significance of self-care for parents. She advocates prioritizing one's well-being, asserting that the quality of time spent with children matters more than quantity. As a parent, you will make mistakes but give yourself grace. She reassures listeners that striving for perfection isn't necessary, but continuous growth is attainable.
Tune in to gain insights from Anna's candid experiences and practical parenting tips.
