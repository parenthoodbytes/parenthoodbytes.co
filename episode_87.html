Episode 87: Ivy Brooks - Journey into Tech & Marketing

Episode Notes

In this episode, we sit down with Ivy Brooks, who is a Business and Marketing Strategist, published author, and professional celebrant. Ivy partners with mission-driven individuals to help them create lasting, meaningful impact in both their industries and personal lives.
Based in Las Vegas and raising a nine-year-old son, Ivy shares her experience of launching and growing her own business while navigating the complexities of single motherhood. She opens up about the realities of balancing entrepreneurship and parenting, the moments of doubt, and the mindset shifts that helped her stay aligned with her values.
We also explore her unexpected journey into the tech and marketing space, how she built her expertise, and the ways she continues to evolve as both a strategist and a parent.
