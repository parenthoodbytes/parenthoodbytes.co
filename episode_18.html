Episode 18: Ahmed Siddiqui

Episode Notes

In this podcast episode, Ahmed Siddiqui, author of Anatomy of the Swipe, instructor for Academy of the Swipe, and a Product Leader at Branch, shares insights into his journey balancing career and family life. With three children aged 10, 8, and 4, Ahmed reflects on how his approach to work has evolved since becoming a parent over a decade ago. He emphasizes the importance of being intentional in managing work-life balance, particularly in prioritizing time with his children.
Ahmed acknowledges the challenges of staying present with his kids amidst professional responsibilities, highlighting the struggle to maintain a balance of time. He underscores the significance of consciously putting away distractions like smartphones to fully engage with family.
While the transition to parenthood can be daunting, Ahmed reassures listeners that it's a natural part of the journey. Moreover, he shares insights into the dynamics of having multiple children, noting that while it may seem like more work, the experience is ultimately enriching. Ahmed emphasizes the importance of nurturing the relationship with one's partner amidst the demands of parenting multiple children, advocating for intentional time spent together.
