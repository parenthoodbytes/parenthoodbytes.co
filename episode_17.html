Episode 17: Emily Price

Episode Notes

In this episode, Emily Price, a Staff Technical Product Manager at Singularity 6 and a mother of two young children, aged 5 and 2, shares her insights on navigating career expectations and childcare responsibilities after becoming a parent.
Emily emphasizes the need to adjust and reset expectations regarding one's job after having children. She offers valuable advice for parents, highlighting the importance of open communication and alignment with everyone involved in childcare planning. Recognizing that different individuals may have varying expectations regarding childcare arrangements, Emily stresses the significance of reaching a consensus on what these arrangements entail.
Furthermore, Emily suggests setting daily intentions to stay focused and productive amidst parental responsibilities. She advocates for taking time at the end of each day to reflect and cultivate a mindset of gratitude and calmness. Regarding childcare logistics, Emily advises parents to proactively join waitlists and submit applications early to secure childcare options effectively.
