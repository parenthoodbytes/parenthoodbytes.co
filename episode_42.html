Episode 42: Tali Gutman

Episode Notes

In this episode, we sit down with Tali Gutman, a staff software engineer at Datadog based in Colorado. Tali, who has two young children, ages 2 and 4, opens up about the unique challenges of having a baby during the pandemic, her career journey into tech, and how she manages to balance work with parental leave and family life. She shares her experiences with parental leave, emphasizing its significance, and discusses the dynamics of having both parents working in the tech industry.
Tune in to hear her honest reflections and firsthand experiences!
