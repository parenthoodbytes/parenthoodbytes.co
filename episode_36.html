Episode 36: Dave White

Episode Notes

In this episode, we talk to Dave White, who was formerly the Head of Product Operations and is currently an Operator and Investor as well as an advisor in Product Management in the Bay Area. He In this episode, we talk to Dave White, who was formerly the Head of Product Operations and is currently an Operator and Investor as well as an advisor in Product Management in the Bay Area.
Dave shares insights from his personal life, focusing on the challenges of raising children when both parents have demanding careers. He discusses the importance of balancing responsibilities and managing time effectively, suggesting that parents should find time to exercise as a means of relaxation. Additionally, he emphasizes the value of investing in a 529 plan for children's education. Dave highlights that one of the most significant and challenging aspects of parenting is to help kids be themselves and grow up in the way that's best for their makeup.
