Episode 26: Mei

Episode Notes

In this episode, we chat with with Mei, a data scientist based in Singapore. She shares her experiences of parenting two young children, aged 3.5 years old and 4 months old. Mei candidly discusses the struggles and challenges she faces while juggling her career and family life.
One key piece of advice Mei offers to fellow parents, whether they are planning to have children or already have them, is the importance of sharing at-home challenges with coworkers. Mei emphasizes the value of making colleagues aware of the difficulties parents may be navigating.
Tune in to gain insights from Mei's journey through parenthood.
