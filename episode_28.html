Episode 28: Vicky Fang

Episode Notes

In this episode, we catch up with Vicky Fang, a parent of two kids aged 8 and 10, who shares her journey from a tech career to becoming a children's book author and illustrator. She discusses the challenges of transitioning careers and how parenthood has influenced her approach to work efficency and prioritization. Vicky offers candid insights into the realities of parenting, acknowledging both the expected and unexpected aspects.
Vicky's diverse range of books caters to children of all ages, starting as young as newborns. To check out her books, visit her website at www.vickyfang.com or check out her books on Amazon at https://www.amazon.com/Vicky-Fang/e/B07YH9YYQX.
