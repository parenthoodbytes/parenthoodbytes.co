Episode 25: Susan Lee Neth

Episode Notes

In this episode, we have the pleasure of interviewing Susan Lee Neth, a Software Engineer at LaunchDarkly. She is based in the Bay Area and also serves as a Girl Scout Troop Leader. Susan is a mother of three children, aged 18, 16, and 14. Throughout our conversation, Susan highlights the significance of establishing boundaries in the workplace and prioritizing activities outside of work that hold personal importance.
One key point Susan emphasizes is the importance of inquiring about work benefits before accepting a job offer. Understanding the level of flexibility a job offers can greatly impact one's ability to balance work and personal life effectively. She shares a valuable tip about advocating for oneself by allowing others to accommodate your needs and inviting them to be a part of your journey.
Overall, Susan's insights shed light on the importance of maintaining a healthy work-life balance and advocating for oneself in the workplace. Her experiences as both a software engineer and a parent offer valuable lessons for anyone striving to navigate the demands of career and family life effectively.
