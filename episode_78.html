Episode 78: Nam - Raising Kids, Navigating the Pandemic, and Career Transitions

Episode Notes

In this episode, we chat with Nam, Director of Product at Oracle and father of two (ages 4 and 1). He shares his journey into the tech world, the unique experience of navigating parenthood during the pandemic, and his thoughtful transition out of the demanding consultant lifestyle.
Nam offers reassuring wisdom for parents, encouraging them to embrace the present, avoid over-planning, and trust that their perspectives will naturally evolve as their children grow.
